  • 👋 Greetings!

    • I'm currently a final-year PhD student in the esteemed Department of Software Systems and Cybersecurity at Monash University.

    In response to the ever-growing cybersecurity challenges in our digital realm, my research is dedicated to equipping developers with cutting-edge tools that proactively address security concerns within the code itself. A tangible outcome of this effort is AIBugHunter, a deep learning-based software security tool now available as an open-source solution in the VSCode Marketplace. Boasting over 500 downloads, AIBugHunter stands out for its ability to detect and locate line-level vulnerabilities, identify vulnerability types (CWE-IDs), estimate CVSS severity, and suggest automatic repair patches directly within developers' IDEs.

    Beyond academic recognition through publications in prestigious venues like ICSE, FSE, and TSE, our work has made a significant impact on the broader community. AIBugHunter has not only garnered attention within the academic sphere but has also been featured in reputable Australian media outlets, including coverage in the Australian Cybersecurity Magazine.

    I am originally from Taipei, Taiwan. I currently live in Melbourne, Australia with my partner.
